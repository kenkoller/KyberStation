# YouTube Outreach — Cold Emails

Five variants, pick by recipient type. Send **one at a time, manually
personalized**. The "[mention specific video]" bracket is non-negotiable
— if you can't fill that in honestly, don't send the email.

Track each outreach so you know who's been contacted, when, and where

**Hard rules:**
- Don't mass-email. Personalize every one.
- Don't ask for a review or shoutout in the first message.
- Don't promise revenue / sponsorship / affiliate codes — KyberStation is free.
- Don't follow up more than once. Two emails total per recipient, max.

**Send window:** 7-14 days after Reddit launch, once you have real user
feedback to mention ("the community has already helped me fix X").

## Personalization checklist (fill in BEFORE sending)

For every recipient, verify or research:

1. [ ] **Specific video / tutorial / review** — not "I love your channel"
2. [ ] **Specific detail from that video** — proves you actually watched
3. [ ] **Their saber board(s) or hardware** — adjust the hardware claim
4. [ ] **Their content focus** — pick the right Variant (1-5)
5. [ ] **Their preferred channel** — email vs. YouTube about page vs.
       Patreon DM vs. business inquiry form. Use what they list.

If you can't fill #1-2 honestly, **don't send the email.** A generic
template hurts you more than it helps.
